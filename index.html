Enjoy a Dream Vaccation In The Vila Alushi

Offering city views, Vila Alushi is an accommodation located in Sarandë, 2.1 km from Flamingo Beach and 13 km from Butrint National Park. This property offers access to a balcony, free private parking and free WiFi. The property is non-smoking and is set 1.9 km from Mango Beach.
